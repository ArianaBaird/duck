# Vibe Coding Assignment
## Duck Party Game (Original Prompt)
### By: Ariana Baird

Create a demo app using the p5.js library


Make a 2D duck:

Draw a circle in the middle of the screen with a diameter of ⅛ of the window width. Make that circle the body of a cute plump (fat) duck, with no neck, just a circle head, the eyes should look like the pusheen plushies and the beak should look like this bracket ( but rotated 90 degrees right. The body composition should be similar to a peppa pig character, and the feet should be half circles with the flat side facing down. There should be one wing on each side. The colours of the duck should start to look similar to a white goose. On top of the circle head should be a hat, this hat should start off as a party hat with a pink base and a yellow pom pom.

Functions of the duck/ducks:

When the enter key is pressed the duck should move in a random direction linearly, at a visible pace, but when pressed again the duck/ducks should stop moving, until the enter key is pressed again.

While the duck is making its way towards the edge of the screen the enter duck should gradually rotate so that the feet are perpendicular to the edge of the screen by the time the duck reaches the edge of the screen.

When the feet of the duck reach the edge of the screen the duck should bounce off in a random linear direction that would allow the duck to have time to gradually rotate before hitting the edge of the screen again.

When the feet of the duck hit the edge of the screen or when the “p” key is pressed: make the sound of a party blower and make rectangular confetti of assorted colours of the rainbow explode in the middle of the screen , then fall towards the bottom each moving at different speeds and in different directions, once the piece of confetti reaches the bottom of the screen it disappears.

When any number key is pressed, add that number of ducks to the screen, each an exact replica of the current duck, except for the placement, when a new duck is added, put it in a random spot on the screen.

When the spacebar is pressed, reduce the amount of ducks back to one.


When the “h” key is pressed, change the type of hat on the ducks head/heads, rotate between the party hat, a top hat with a black base and red middle thing, baseball cap that’s blue, and a winter hat/ a tuke that is red.



When the mouse clicks the duck, play a “quack” / duck sound one time.

When the “d” key is pressed the colours of the duck should rotate to being, similar to a Canadian goose, a yellow duck, a mallard, and the original white goose.

When the “g” key is pressed add grass to the bottom, rotate between the look of plain grass in peppa pig, the grass with flowers from peppa pig, the grass with a muddy puddle from peppa pig, the grass with a pond from peppa pig, and nothing.


Formatting of The Screen:

The background of the screen should be baby blue, (137,207,240)

In the top right corner of the screen make a legend, with the background of white, stroke weight of the box being 2, the font being Quicksand from google docs, the top being 1/40 of the window width away from the top of the screen and the right side being 1/40 of the window width away from the right side of the screen. Make there be no title of the legend. The first thing stated in the legend should be “[enter] - stop / start” the second, “[space] reset # of ducks” the third, “(1,2,3…) - add __ ducks” the fourth, “d - duck change” the fifth, “g - grass” the sixth, “h - hat change” the seventh, “p - party!” the eight, “click the duck - ???”
